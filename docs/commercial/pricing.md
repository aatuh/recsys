# Pricing

Pricing details were recovered from the previous `.trash/docs` commercial references and condensed for this restarted
documentation set. Signed order forms override this page.

All prices are EUR, excluding VAT.

## Plan definitions

| Plan | Price | Scope | Default support |
| --- | ---: | --- | --- |
| Commercial Evaluation | EUR 490 one-time | 30 days, 1 tenant, 1 deployment, non-production evaluation. | Best-effort async, no SLA. |
| Starter | EUR 9,900 per year | 1 tenant, 1 production deployment plus up to 2 non-prod environments, 1-2 production recommendation surfaces. | Best-effort async, no SLA. |
| Growth | EUR 24,900 per year | Up to 3 tenants and/or production deployments, up to 6 production recommendation surfaces. | Async first-response target within 2 business days, no SLA unless purchased. |
| Enterprise | From EUR 60,000 per year | Custom scope for OEM/resale, regulated deployments, multi-region HA, custom SLA, or custom legal/security terms. | Defined in the signed order form. |

## Fixed-scope services

| Package | Price | Scope |
| --- | ---: | --- |
| Pilot Integration Review | EUR 5,000 one-time | Review one scoped pilot integration, instrumentation, tenant/surface setup, and evaluation readiness. |
| Production Readiness Package | EUR 12,500 one-time | Review deployment shape, rollback runbooks, hardening checklist, observability, and production cutover risks. |
| Security / Procurement Review Package | EUR 5,000 one-time | Support security/procurement review with artifact navigation and hardening checklist review. |
| Premium support / SLA | Custom | 8x5 or 24x7 response commitments for Growth or Enterprise, captured in the order form. |
